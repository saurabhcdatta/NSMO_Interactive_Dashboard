WHAT IS THIS?
-------------
The NSMO Dashboard is an interactive tool for exploring
mortgage outcomes data — including rate comparisons,
borrower demographics, and lending patterns between
credit unions and other lenders.

------------------------------------------------------------

FIRST-TIME SETUP (do this once only)
-------------------------------------

Step 1.  Copy the entire NSMO_Dashboard folder from the
         network drive to your Desktop.

         The folder is large (~500MB). Copying may take
         2–5 minutes depending on your connection speed.

         !! IMPORTANT: Do NOT run the app directly from
         the network drive. Always copy it locally first.

Step 2.  Open the NSMO_Dashboard folder on your Desktop.

Step 3.  Double-click  launch.bat

Step 4.  A black window will appear — this is normal.
         Wait 20–30 seconds.

Step 5.  The dashboard will open automatically in your
         default web browser.

         If your browser does not open automatically,
         copy this address into your browser manually:
         http://127.0.0.1:6734

------------------------------------------------------------

EVERY TIME AFTER THAT
----------------------

Step 1.  Open NSMO_Dashboard on your Desktop.
Step 2.  Double-click launch.bat.
Step 3.  Wait 20–30 seconds for the browser to open.

         !! IMPORTANT: Keep the black window open while
         using the dashboard. Closing it stops the app.

------------------------------------------------------------

IF YOU SEE A SECURITY WARNING
------------------------------

Windows may show a blue or grey warning screen saying
"Windows protected your PC."

This is normal for apps not downloaded from the internet.

  1. Click "More info"
  2. Click "Run anyway"

If you do not see "Run anyway," contact IT and ask them
to whitelist the NSMO_Dashboard folder.

------------------------------------------------------------

COMMON PROBLEMS
---------------

Problem:  Black window appears then immediately closes.
Fix:      Make sure you copied the full folder including
          the R-4.x.x subfolder. Re-copy from the
          network drive if anything is missing.

Problem:  Browser opens but shows an error page.
Fix:      Close the black window, wait 10 seconds,
          then double-click launch.bat again.

Problem:  Port 6734 is already in use.
Fix:      The launcher will automatically try port 6735.
          Use http://127.0.0.1:6735 in your browser.

Problem:  Dashboard is very slow.
Fix:      You may be running it from the network drive.
          Copy the folder to your Desktop and try again.

Problem:  Any other issue.
Fix:      Contact [YOUR NAME] at [YOUR EMAIL].
          Please include a screenshot of any error message.

------------------------------------------------------------

UPDATING THE DASHBOARD
-----------------------

When a new version is available:

  1. Delete the NSMO_Dashboard folder from your Desktop.
  2. Copy the new version from the network drive.
  3. Launch as normal.

You will be notified by email when updates are available.
